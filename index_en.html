Megra d.o.o is a family company with a long tradition and many years of experience in the construction of high quality residential and commercial buildings. In our working life, we have led numerous projects in all its phases as contractors, supervising engineers and investors.

We build in accordance with the highest standards in architecture and construction while respecting the needs and requirements of our clients. When performing works, we use the latest technologies, and we demand the same from our subcontractors and associates with very strict quality control.

Our goals are world trends in real estate development, application of the highest quality materials and professional project management. Today, our company works on many smaller and larger facilities throughout Croatia in accordance with demanding building regulations and laws.

About us


Construction company Megra d.o.o. was founded in 1993 in Donji Kraljevec. It is domestically owned, and its activity is the execution of construction works for building and civil engineering, as well as finishing works in construction. To date, the company has built a large number of facilities of various scales and purposes for domestic and foreign investors. Megra d.o.o. has its headquarters in Donji Kraljevec and currently has 10 high-quality construction workers who can perform all demanding projects on the orders and wishes of investors. The company is equipped with modern construction machinery, tools and equipment, which requires fast and quality execution of even the most demanding construction works on new construction, adaptation, reconstruction and maintenance of constructed facilities.
